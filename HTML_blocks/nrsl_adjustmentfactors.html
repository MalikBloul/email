Adjustment Factors

If you're applying for a Macquarie University degree through UAC, you may be eligible to receive adjustment factors – additional points that can be used to boost your selection rank.

Adjustment Factors help to get you over the line when your previous studies don't meet the cut off for your dream degree! You can receive a maximum of either 5, 8, 10, 12, or 15 additional points combined across six Adjustment Factor schemes, depending on your degrees of interest.  Points may be awarded based on achievement in Academia (Big History Scheme) or in extra-curricular activities (Elite Athletes and Performers Scheme). You may also be eligible for points based on where you live (Macquarie Catchment; Regional Entry Scheme), or due to educational or social disadvantage (Educational Access Scheme). Some adjustment factors are automatically applied, while others require you to submit an application. Please check each adjustment factor page for the correct process.
