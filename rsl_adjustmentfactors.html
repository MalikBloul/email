Adjustment Factors

If you are in Year 12 completing the HSC (or equivalent) or the International Baccalaureate (IB) and you’ve included Macquarie in your preferences, you may be eligible to receive adjustment factors – additional points that can be used to boost your selection rank.

Adjustment Factors help to get you over the line when your high school marks don't meet the cut off for your dream degree!  You can receive a maximum of either 5, 8, 10, 12, or 15 additional points combined across eight Adjustment Factor schemes, depending on your degrees of interest.  Points may be awarded based on achievement in Academia (Academic Advantage; Big History Scheme) or in extra-curricular activities (Elite Athletes and Performers Scheme; Duke of Edinburgh, Queen's Scout and Queen's Guide Awards Scheme).  You may also be eligible for points based on where you live (Macquarie Catchment; Regional Entry Scheme), or due to educational or social disadvantage (Educational Access Scheme).  Some adjustment factors are automatically applied, while others require you to submit an application. Please check each adjustment factor page for the correct process.
