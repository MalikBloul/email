How to Apply

As a non-recent school leaver, entry into the {course_name} is based on your selection rank which is made up of any or all of the following: 

  • Tertiary studies (including your GPA or WAM) 

  • Secondary studies (e.g., ATAR or equivalent) 

  • Adjustment Factor Points 

  • Post-secondary studies (e.g., TAFE – Certificate IV or higher) 

You will need to provide your final secondary school marks and any other studies you have completed as part of your application. These will be used to determine whether you meet the requirements for entry into your preferred degree at Macquarie. The Universities Admissions Centre (UAC) will then generate a selection rank based on these results, which Macquarie will use to assess your application. The selection rank for the {course_name} is {selection_rank}.

Please keep in mind that the higher your previous tertiary marks are, the more competitive your application is likely to be. 

Applications for undergraduate study should be made through UAC, using the code {uac_code}. {key_dates} Applications are assessed on a rolling basis and application dates can be found on the UAC Key Dates.
